Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 22, 2026 | Version 2.0

📌 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to schools, teachers, parents, and students:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address
  • School Data: Student records, attendance, grades, fees
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited
  • Device Information: OS, device type, app version

⚙️ 2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us to:

  • ✓ Provide and maintain SchoolERP services
  • ✓ Process fee payments and generate reports
  • ✓ Improve user experience and AI-driven insights
  • ✓ Send important notifications and updates
  • ✓ Ensure security and prevent fraud

🔒 3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • 🔐 AES-256 encryption for sensitive data
  • 🛡️ Spring Security framework
  • 📋 Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • 👤 Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • 🌐 Secure API with HTTPS/TLS 1.3
💡 Note: Your data is stored on secure servers in India (GDPR compliant).

🍪 4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience. Types of cookies we use:

Cookie Type Purpose
Essential Login sessions, security
Analytics Usage patterns, performance
Preferences Language, theme settings

🔄 5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell your personal data. However, we may share data with:

  • 🏦 Payment gateways (Razorpay, PayU) for fee collection
  • 📧 Email service providers for notifications
  • ⚖️ Legal authorities if required by law
  • 🤝 Service providers who help us operate SchoolERP

👶 6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

SchoolERP is used by educational institutions. We collect student data only with parental consent or under school authority. Parents/guardians can request access or deletion of their child's data.

📁 7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request data deletion at any time. Backup data is retained for 30 days after deletion request.

🌍 8. Your Rights (GDPR & Indian IT Act)

You have the following rights:

  • ✅ Right to Access: See what data we have about you
  • ✅ Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • ✅ Right to Erasure: Request data deletion (Right to be forgotten)
  • ✅ Right to Portability: Download your data
  • ✅ Right to Object: Opt-out of marketing communications

🤖 9. AI-Driven Modules & Privacy

Our AI modules use anonymized data to generate insights. Personal identifiers are removed before AI processing. You can opt-out of AI features in your account settings.

🔗 10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (payment gateways, help centers). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing information.

📝 11. Policy Updates & Notifications

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via:

  • ✉️ Email notification to registered users
  • 📢 In-app notification
  • 🏠 Notice on our website homepage

Continued use of SchoolERP after changes means acceptance of the updated policy.
